Welcome to Guided Meditation

Guided meditation is a form of meditation that utilizes several different techniques to achieve or enhance the meditative state. It may simply be meditation done under the guidance of a trained practitioner or teacher and may include the use of imagery or music. Guided meditation can be helpful for people who are trying to meditate for the first time. The benefits of meditation extend far beyond relaxation. It can help reduce anxiety, depression, insomnia, general pain, and high blood pressure. The videos on this page will provide you with resources on how to get started!

Guided Meditation for Positive Energy

This video is a guided meditation video made by Aileen, a lifestyle creator who created Lavendaire, a resource for personal growth and lifestyle design. The purpose of this video is to help you relax and allow your mind to be ready for positive energy, relaxation, and peace. In this 20 minute video, soothing music is played while Aileen guides you through the meditation. Meditation can be extremely helpful when it comes to finding energy for the day. If you're feeling down and low energy, this guided meditation video may help you calm your mind and allow for positive energy to flow into your mind.

Guided Meditation for a Relaxed Body and Mind

This video is a guided meditation video from the YouTube channel Great Meditation. This video is one of many guided meditation videos offered by this channel. The purpose of this video is to help both your body and mind to feel relaxed. Throughout the 15 minute video, the narrator guides you through the steps of meditation while peaceful music plays in the background. Often, we may all feel anxious and stressed because of everything that is going on in our lives, but it is important to take some time to fully relax and allow both our physical and mental states to recover.
