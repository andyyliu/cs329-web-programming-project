Welcome to Yoga

Yoga is a group of physical, mental, and spiritual practices or disciplines that originated in ancient India. In current times, yoga often refers to an exercise that consists of postures or asanas. Yoga is an exercise that can help bring together the mind and body. It incorporates breathing exercises, meditation, and poses that will help encourage relaxation and reduce stress. Yoga can also reduce inflammation, improve heart health, fight depression, reduce chronic pain, increase sleep quality, improve breathing and flexibility, relieve migraines, and increase strength.

Energizing Power Vinyasa Flow

This video is a guided yoga video taught Briohny Smyth, a yoga teacher. This type of yoga is called the Power Vinyasa Flow. It is a powerful, energetic form of yoga that allows you to fluidly move from one pose to the next while connecting your breathing to your movements. It is fast-paced and focuses on building strength and endurance. Although this still requires mindfulness and focus on breathing, power yoga is more dynamic than other meditative forms of yoga, which means it is a good cardio and strength workout. In this 30 minute video, Smyth will perform each pose and exercise with you, as well as narrate the actions required.

Yoga for Anxiety and Stress

This video is a guided yoga video taught by Adriene. In this video, Adriene will guide you through breath and body practices that serve you when you feel bad. These breathing techniques and spinal work will assist you in moments of stress. In this 30 minute video, Adriene guides you through yoga practices that will help alleviate anxiety and stress. According to multiple studies, yoga has been shown to help people reduce anxiety and stress. If you are feeling overwhelmed or uncertain, take some time out of your day to practice some yoga. It could make a big difference in boosting your mood!
